about us

⭕ Welcome to Doramadala Resort, where tranquility meets luxury. Nestled in the heart of Jaffna Road,Anuradhapura our resort is a haven of serenity surrounded by breathtaking natural beauty. With a rich history and a commitment to providing unparalleled hospitality, Doramadala Resort is more than just a getaway; it's an experience.

⭕ Our resort offers a perfect blend of modern comfort and traditional charm, ensuring that every guest feels at home. Whether you're here for a relaxing vacation, a special event, or a corporate retreat, our dedicated team is here to make your stay unforgettable. Immerse yourself in the unique allure of Doramadala Resort, where every moment is crafted for your pleasure.
